This mod uses some exported sounds from the Xbox 360 NXE Dashboard, which was used
from around 2007/2008 to 2011, when the Metro dashboard was introduced.
Included in v3.0 of this mod is:
- Keyboard sounds
- Other sound effects
- Correctly formatted CSS themes for dark and light
- Custom animated background
- Background Music
- Xbox 360 Font
- Custom Splash Screen

KNOWN BUGS:
- Setting the volume to 200 will peak the audio of the sound effects. PLEASE
USE DEFAULT VOLUME FOR THE LOVE OF GOD.
